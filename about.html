Our Mission: A Tribute to Mahatma Gandhi

“A clean India would be the best tribute India could pay to Mahatma Gandhi on his 150 birth anniversary in 2019,” said Shri Narendra Modi as he launched the Swachh Bharat Mission at Rajpath in New Delhi. On 2nd October 2014, Swachh Bharat Mission was launched throughout length and breadth of the country as a national movement. The campaign aims to achieve the vision of a ‘Clean India’ by 2nd October 2019.

At EndplasticWaste.com, we are inspired by this vision and are committed to making a significant contribution to a cleaner India and a greener world. Our mission is to raise awareness about the detrimental effects of plastic waste on our environment and to encourage responsible and sustainable practices that reduce plastic usage and pollution.

Join us in this endeavor as we strive for a cleaner, plastic-free India and a future that honours Mahatma Gandhi's legacy.
